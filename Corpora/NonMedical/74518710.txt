L'École de la chambre syndicale de la couture parisienne is a private institution of higher education in the creative professions founded in 1927 by the Chambre Syndicale de la Haute Couture.It provides training in fashion and haute couture techniques and has been located in the 2nd arrondissement of Paris since 2010. As of 2019, the École de la chambre syndicale de la couture parisienne and the IFM, founded in 1986, have merged to create the new Institut français de la mode.


== Famous graduates ==
